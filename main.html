About Chiguru

Chiguru is our annual cultural fest, filled with music, dance, art, and fun events to celebrate creativity and talent. It provides a vibrant platform for students to showcase their skills, express their passions, and build unforgettable memories. From soulful singing and energetic dance performances to thrilling competitions and artistic showcases, Chiguru brings together diversity, unity, and youthful spirit. It is not just a fest but a celebration of culture, innovation, and togetherness, where every participant and audience member experiences the true essence of college life.
